# Kipepeo Intelligence 🦋
### Humanizing Data for Grassroots Financial Ecosystems

Kipepeo Intelligence is an AI-powered financial intelligence platform designed to predict creditworthiness for the informal economy. It translates alternative data—such as M-Pesa transaction patterns, Fuliza repayment speeds, and utility consistency—into institutional-grade resilience scores.

## 🚀 The Problem
Over 80% of the Kenyan workforce operates in the informal economy. These individuals are financially active, discipline-driven, and resilient, yet they are often locked out of formal credit because traditional scoring models do not "speak" the language of grassroots finance (SMS logs, Sheng-nuanced transactions, and Chama contributions).

## 💡 Our Solution
Kipepeo uses the **Google Gemini 1.5 & Flash** models to parse raw, unformatted financial logs. By analyzing behavioral patterns rather than just balance sheets, we provide:
- **Resilience Score (300-850):** A creditworthiness metric based on alternative data.
- **Behavioral Mapping:** Translating Sheng and raw SMS logs into financial intent (e.g., "Stocking," "Emergency Bridge," "Social Capital").
- **Financial Health Reports:** Professional verification certificates for side-hustles and Chamas to present to lenders or partners.

## 🏗️ Technical Architecture
- **Frontend:** React 18 + Vite for a high-performance SPA experience.
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS for a clean, "Technical/Modern" aesthetic.
- **AI Intelligence:** `@google/genai` integration using Gemini 3 Flash Preview for real-time parsing and sentiment analysis.
- **Data Visualization:** Recharts for mapping financial resilience and market reach.
- **Animations:** Framer Motion for intuitive route transitions and interactive UI elements.
- **Deployment:** Optimized for Cloud Run / AI Studio with a custom Node.js/Express server wrapper.

## 🛠️ How to Use
1. **Ingest Data:** Navigate to the **Ingestion Hub** and paste raw transaction logs (M-Pesa, KPLC, Fuliza).
2. **AI Analysis:** Click "Run AI Analysis." Gemini will parse the entries to identify income, expenditure, and repayment velocity.
3. **Review Dashboard:** View your generated **Resilience Score** and detailed behavioral insights.
- **Audit Reports:** Generate a printable **Health Report** to serve as a proof-of-resilience certificate.

## 🇰🇪 Localized Intelligence
The platform is specifically tuned for the Kenyan context, understanding:
- **Fuliza Behavior:** Identifying "Business Bridge" patterns (fast repayment for inventory).
- **Utility Consistency:** Using KPLC token frequency to prove residential or business stability.
- **Linguistic Nuance:** Parsing Sheng-mixed logs (e.g., "Kupiga stock," "Lipa debt," "Chama funds").
